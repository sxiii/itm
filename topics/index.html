Topics

25 January 2024

There are a wide list of topics that we're ready to discuss and cover on our events.

Topic focuses (list can and will be extended, so please contact us if you have a different talk anyway):

  • Crypto, Blockchain, FinTech & DAO
  • Startups, Pitches, Kickstarting, Crowdfunding, Ideas
  • Business Improvement, Success Stories, Challenges, Management Efficiency, etc.
  • Open-source Software, Linux & Unix, Development stacks, Programming languages
  • Open-source Hardware, Development, Use-cases
  • AI, NN, Language Models
  • Computer Game Design, Board Game Development
  • Cities of the future, futurology
  • Energy Sector
  • Communications
  • Consulting & Advising & Legal
  • Design, Architecture
  • Press, Journalism
  • Agriculture
  • Medical tech
  • Biology and bio-engineering, implants, sextech
  • Education & Meetups
  • Content production - movies, videos, audio, singing (making, editing)

Our events might be interesting for you, if you're one of these guys:

  • Software & hardware electronics enthusiast
  • Geek & nerd in one of the above mentioned fields
  • Prefer to open-source everything
  • Interested in AI & Robotics
  • Making own business or interested in becoming an entrepreneur
  • Looking into the future
