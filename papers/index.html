Call for papers

25 January 2024

Read more about format of our event and topics covered at the Topics page.

If you would like to speak or to be added to ITM Knowledge Base, please submit your presentations to papers@itmeet.top with according information (Date, Topic, Links, Documents, Handouts, Requirements, etc).

Each presentation and information paper should be submitted at least 3 days before the event.

Knowledge Base / partners / other documents can be submitted at any time.

We'll do our best to read all the papers and give feedback ASAP after receiving them.

Thanks for your collaboration and looking forward to see you on the event both offline and online!
