International Tech Meetups


Please join on our exciting journey with our first ITM issue, 17 February 2024.

Location: Rebel
Address: Universitetsgata 2, Oslo
Date & Time: 17 February 2024, 17:00
RSVP: on meetup
Map & Route: OpenStreetMap, Google map, Mapiliary, Other maps)
Venue website: rebel.no

There will be up to 4 topics covered. Extra special activities after the main event!

  • Den Ivanov — ITM Community announcement. Who we are, what we plan, how to join and help.
  • Małgorzata Janeczek — Screw perfect, aim for good enough. A brief story of how not to go nuts facing complexity (with a small advice for beginners).
  • Den Ivanov — We need better marketing for open-source software
  • Secret speaker — Check-out our announces soon.
